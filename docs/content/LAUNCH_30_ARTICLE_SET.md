## Niche: Home Office / Remote Work Equipment

- High ticket ($300–$1,500 products)
- High demand (remote work is permanent)
- Huge organic search volume
- Strong affiliate programs (Amazon, direct brand, ShareASale)

**This architecture is niche-agnostic.** Swap chairs for software, desks for tools,
monitors for SaaS — the structure stays identical.

### Review structure (all 8 follow identical format)

1. Quick verdict (50 words)
2. Specs at a glance (table)
3. Who it's for / who it's NOT for
4. Pros (3–5 bullets)
5. Cons (2–3 bullets — real drawbacks)
6. Detailed breakdown (build quality, comfort, adjustability, value)
7. Comparison to alternatives (links to comparison pages)
8. FAQ (4+ questions)
9. Final recommendation with affiliate CTA

### Why these exist

- Targets product-specific search queries (brand + review)
- Highest conversion rate of any page type
- Supports and strengthens comparison pages
- Captures bottom-of-funnel buyers ready to purchase

## Niche Swap Guide

To use this architecture for a different niche:

1. Replace product category (chairs → software, desks → tools, etc.)
2. Identify 3 pillar topics for the new niche
3. Map brand-vs-brand comparisons (4 head-to-head articles)
4. Map budget/use-case roundups (8 articles)
5. Select 8 individual products for deep reviews
6. Identify 5 informational questions in the niche
7. Adapt authority builder pages

The structure, linking, and publishing cadence remain identical.
