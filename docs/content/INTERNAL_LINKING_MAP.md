## Implementation Rules for OpenClaw

1. **Every page MUST link to article 03 (Setup Guide)**
2. **Every review/comparison MUST link to its parent pillar (01 or 02)**
3. **Every review/comparison MUST link to article 29 (Methodology)**
4. **Every page MUST have minimum 3 internal links**
5. **Pillar hubs MUST have 6+ internal links**
6. **Anchor text MUST be descriptive keyword phrases, never "click here"**
7. **When a new article publishes, existing related articles MUST be updated with backlinks to it**
8. **Link placement: in body text (contextual), not just at bottom**
