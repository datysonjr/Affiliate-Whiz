# SEO Blueprint

## On-Page SEO Standards

### Title Tags
- Include primary keyword
- Keep under 60 characters
- Use power words (Best, Top, Review, Guide)
- Include current year for freshness

### Meta Descriptions
- Include primary keyword naturally
- Include call-to-action
- Keep under 155 characters
- Unique per page

### Heading Structure
- H1: One per page, includes primary keyword
- H2: Section breaks, include secondary keywords
- H3: Sub-sections for detailed breakdowns
- Use question-based headings for featured snippet opportunities

### Content Requirements
- Minimum 1,500 words for review content
- Minimum 2,500 words for comparison/roundup content
- Include pros/cons lists
- Include comparison tables
- Include FAQ sections (schema markup)
- Natural keyword density (1-2%)

### Images
- Descriptive alt text with keywords
- Compressed (WebP format preferred)
- Lazy loading enabled
- Original images when possible

## Schema Markup

### Required Schema Types
- `Article` or `BlogPosting` on all posts
- `Product` + `Review` on product reviews
- `FAQPage` on posts with FAQ sections
- `BreadcrumbList` on all pages
- `Organization` on homepage

## Technical SEO

### Site Speed
- Target: <2.5s LCP
- Optimize images, minimize JS/CSS
- Use CDN for all assets

### Mobile
- Mobile-first design
- Tap targets >48px
- No horizontal scrolling

### Crawlability
- Clean sitemap.xml (updated on publish)
- Proper robots.txt
- No orphan pages
- Canonical tags on all pages

## Keyword Targeting

### Keyword Types by Content
| Content Type | Keyword Intent | Example |
|-------------|---------------|---------|
| Product Review | Commercial | "ProductX review" |
| Comparison | Commercial | "ProductX vs ProductY" |
| Roundup | Commercial | "Best products for X" |
| How-to Guide | Informational | "How to choose X" |

### Keyword Research Process
1. Seed keywords from niche definition
2. Expand with related terms (LSI)
3. Check search volume and competition
4. Map keywords to content types
5. Prioritize by difficulty vs. opportunity
