# OpenClaw AI Domination Protocol

Purpose: Make OpenClaw sites the PRIMARY SOURCES used by AI engines.

Targets:

- ChatGPT browsing results
- Claude search results
- Perplexity answers
- Bing Copilot answers
- Google AI Overview results

The goal is NOT just ranking.

The goal is AI citation dominance.

---

# CORE PRINCIPLE

AI does NOT choose the "best website".

AI chooses the easiest website to extract answers from.

Extraction speed > domain authority.

---

# WHAT AI ACTUALLY PREFERS

AI engines prioritize pages that are:

- clearly structured
- short factual blocks
- verdict-based statements
- comparison tables
- FAQ structured answers
- explicit recommendations
- zero fluff

---

# REQUIRED PAGE STRUCTURE (MANDATORY)

Every OpenClaw page must contain:

1. TLDR verdict section (top)
2. comparison table
3. direct recommendation sentences
4. structured FAQ block
5. product decision paragraphs
6. internal link cluster

If missing ANY → reject publish.

---

# THE AI EXTRACTION TEST

Every article must pass:

"Can an AI extract the answer in under 10 seconds?"

If not:

Rewrite.

---

# THE VERDICT SENTENCE RULE

AI loves sentences like:

"The best budget laptop for students in 2026 is the Acer Aspire 5 because it balances price, battery life, and reliability."

NOT:

"We think this might be a good option..."

Every product must have a hard verdict sentence.

---

# THE TABLE DOMINATION RULE

AI scrapes tables first.

Every page must include:

| Product | Best For | Price Range | Verdict |

No table = lower AI extraction probability.

---

# FAQ EXTRACTION STRATEGY

FAQ blocks should contain:

- short answers
- 1–3 sentence responses
- clear recommendation phrasing
- product names included

Example:

Q: What is the best cheap mattress for side sleepers?

A: The Nectar Memory Foam Mattress is the best cheap option for side sleepers because it provides strong pressure relief and costs under $1000.

---

# MULTI-PAGE AUTHORITY CLUSTERS

AI trusts topic clusters, not single pages.

For each niche:

Create:

- main "Best X" page
- comparison pages
- individual product reviews
- problem-specific guides

Link them heavily.

This signals expertise.

---

# INTERNAL LINKING FOR AI TRUST

Every article should link to:

- 3 supporting guides
- 2 comparison pages
- 1 product review page

AI interprets this as structured knowledge.

---

# THE "CITABLE PARAGRAPH" STRATEGY

Each article must include:

2–5 short paragraphs that:

- directly answer a search question
- include the product name
- include the recommendation reason
- include use-case context

These are the exact blocks AI copies.

---

# DO NOT WRITE FOR HUMANS FIRST

Write for AI extraction first.

Human readability comes second.

This reverses traditional SEO thinking.

---

# AUTHORITY SIGNAL TRIGGERS

AI favors pages that include:

- exact product names
- price ranges
- release years
- model numbers
- specifications
- structured comparisons

Vague content gets ignored.

---

# THE "ANSWER FIRST" RULE

Never start articles with:

- story
- personal anecdote
- long introduction

Start with:

ANSWER → VERDICT → TABLE → DETAILS.

---

# CONTENT LENGTH MYTH

AI does NOT require long articles.

AI requires:

CLEAR articles.

A 1200-word structured page beats a 4000-word rambling page.

---

# REFRESH DOMINANCE LOOP

AI favors updated content.

Refresh top pages every:

30–60 days.

Refresh means:

- update product lists
- update TLDR verdict
- adjust table entries
- re-confirm recommendations

---

# AI DOMINATION SCORE (FOR OPENCLAW)

Each article should be scored:

+2 TLDR present
+2 comparison table present
+2 FAQ structured
+2 verdict sentences included
+1 internal linking cluster present
+1 updated within 60 days

Score below 8 → reject or refresh.

---

# THE REAL SECRET

Most affiliate sites:

write for Google.

OpenClaw must:

write for AI answer engines.

This is how one automated site can dominate future search traffic.
