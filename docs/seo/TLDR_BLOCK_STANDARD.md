# OpenClaw TLDR Block Standard

EVERY article must start with this.

---

## Format

### Quick Answer

Best [product type] for [use case] is:

1. Product A — best overall
2. Product B — best budget
3. Product C — best premium

---

## Why This Exists

AI engines scrape:

- top structured answers
- bullet verdict lists
- comparison summaries

NOT long intros.

---

## Rules

TLDR must:

- appear within first 200 words
- contain product names
- contain use-case language
- contain decision language

---

## Fail Conditions

Reject publish if:

- intro paragraph appears before TLDR
- TLDR contains generic fluff
- TLDR lacks named products

---

## Result

TLDR block alone can generate:

- featured snippet wins
- AI answer citations
- high CTR from search
