# Bing AI Performance Tracking

Bing Webmaster Tools includes AI visibility metrics.

These show:

- citations in AI answers
- grounding query triggers
- AI traffic patterns

---

## Setup

1. create Bing Webmaster account
2. import Google Search Console data
3. verify domain

---

## Monitor

Track:

- pages cited by AI
- queries triggering AI answers
- CTR from AI results

---

## OpenClaw Rule

Pages with AI citations:

→ PRIORITY refresh
→ PRIORITY internal linking
→ PRIORITY expansion

These pages are money machines.
