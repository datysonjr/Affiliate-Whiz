# ARTICLE_REFRESH_ENGINE.md

OpenClaw Affiliate Automation System

Purpose: Defines automated article refresh strategy that boosts rankings without creating new content.

Refreshing existing articles is often more powerful than publishing new ones.

---

## Core Principle

Google favors: **maintained content** over static content.

Updated articles often jump rankings quickly.

---

## Refresh Triggers

OpenClaw should schedule refresh when:

### 1. Age Trigger

If:

```
article_age > 45–60 days
```

Queue for refresh evaluation.

### 2. Ranking Plateau Trigger

If:

- article stuck between positions 10–30
- impressions high but clicks low

This means: Google is testing but unconvinced.

**Perfect refresh candidate.**

### 3. Affiliate Product Change Trigger

If:

- product discontinued
- new model released
- price tiers changed

**Immediate refresh required.**

---

## Refresh Actions

Refresh must NOT rewrite entire article.

Instead:

### Update Intro

Add:

- updated year reference
- new product mention if relevant

### Expand Product Sections

Add:

- new pros/cons
- updated comparisons
- improved buyer guidance

### Expand FAQ

Add:

- 1–2 new buyer questions
- recent concerns
- new compatibility notes

### Add Internal Links

Refresh must include:

- new sibling pages
- new support pages

### Update Publish Timestamp

When refresh complete:

**update visible "last updated" date.**

---

## Refresh Frequency

```
money pages         → every 30–45 days
support pages       → every 90 days
informational pages → every 120+ days
```

---

## Final Rule

**Updating winning pages beats writing new ones.**
